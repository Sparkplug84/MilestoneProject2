Order It!

Welcome to Order it!

Order It! is a fun and interactive quiz where you need to put the answers in the correct order.

Instructions: Once you click the 'START THE QUIZ' button below the first question will appear with 5 answers. Using the buttons you need to put the answers in the correct order. Once your're satisfied with the order click the 'CHECK ANSWERS' button and your score along with your correct answers will be revealed. There are 20 questions to have a go at. Good luck!
